Movie Battle

Battle your partner for a chance to select the movie of you choice.

How To Play

  1. This is a two player game.
  2. Each player will alternate answering a question.
  3. If the you get a question correct you will receive one point.
  4. The player with the highest score gets to pick the movie of their choice.
  5. You have 1 minute to answer the questions
  6. Click the start button to begin.
