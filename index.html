Movie Battle

Battle your partner for a chance to select the movie of your choice!

How To Play

  1. Grab a partner.
  2. You will have 15 seconds to answer each questions.
  3. Click the name of the player who gets the answer first to add a point for that player.
  4. If time runs out no one gets a point.
  5. Whoever has the most points after 10 questions gets to choose which movie to watch!
  6. Click the start button to begin.
